About Us

With the pause of Pokemon OP due to the pandemic, Pokemon Sundays at Odyssey Games is about to get heavy! Compete in a season long league to become the very best!

  • Competitive format for beginners and seasoned players! Grow as you play to make your first step in the competitive world of Pokemon TCG
  • Run along side Pokemon Sundays so players will not need to stress to play in the league as this is purely optional for the competitive players
  • Great benefits for players

Starting with Season 1, players will for 8-10 weeks to earn their spot for the play-offs where they will face off for the trophy and bragging rights (as well as cool prizes!) to show them who is the best in Pasadena.

Why play in the Odyssey League?

  • 01 Competitive Format

    Play in a heavy competitive format to become a better player. Learn how competitive pokemon works to help you in your journey to become a Pokemon World Champion.

  • 02 Anybody can play!

    Even if you're a beginner, a returning player or a seasoned veteran, Odyssey League will give everyone an oppoturnity to play so that you can improve as a player.

  • 03 Prizes, prizes, and more prizes!

    No matter how you do in the tournament, if you are on a losing streak or the league winner, you will always get some sort of reward. Prizes for everyone!

  • 04 Affordable to play

    One time fee of $30 per season will net you some cool player benefits (listed below) and the more players that join the more prizes we will be able to give out!
